Kleebtronics Film Reel

This is a selection of short films and music videos that I had the opportunity to be a part of in one way or another.

Death By Audio in Stop Motion

created by: Mark Kleback and Ryan Viglizzo

This stop-motion animation was made for the Comm Lab: Animation class at ITP. It consists of three interviews with members of the Death By Audio art collective. Special thanks to Edan Wilber, Matt Conboy, and Oliver Ackermann for the interviews and Alexander Christenson for editing.

Clintongore - Watch Out

Shot by Mark Kleeb and Phan Visutyothapibel
Edited by Mark Kleeb and Luisa Covaria

Official Clintongore Website

Description

We shot this video for the band Clintongore using a Canon 5D and a green screen. Editing was done using Adobe After Effects and Final Cut Pro. "Watch Out" is the first single from Clintongore's debut self-titled release.

The Job

written and directed by: Mark Kleback, Matt London, Crys Moore, and Luis Palacios.

The Job is a short horror film made for the Comm Lab: Video and Sound class at ITP. It stars Paul Kite and Jay Heiselmann. Music by A Place To Bury Strangers, Grooms, and French Miami.
