The Job

written and directed by: Mark Kleback, Matt London, Crys Moore, and Luis Palacios.

The Job is a short horror film made for the Comm Lab: Video and Sound class at ITP. It stars Paul Kite and Jay Heiselmann. Music by A Place To Bury Strangers, Grooms, and French Miami.
