Kleebtronics Megaphono
Thesis Research

Crowd Sourced Music

Type 1

Pre-composed music intended for thousands around the globe

Advantages

  • More control over composition
  • More likely to filter out talent
  • Can scale to thousands of users

Disadvantages

  • Completely virtual
  • No personal interaction

Interfaces Built For Non-Musicians

Design physical interfaces that can play music regardless of the musical talent of the player:

Advantages

  • Anyone can operate (even, and especially, kids!)
  • Pre-composed sounds or samples can be designed to sound good
  • Easy to install in physical space

Disadvantages

  • Not actually 'playing' music, just pressing buttons
  • More of an installation than a performance

Interfaces Designed for Musicians

Familiar instruments for musicians to play together, linked by MIDI or other basic protocol.

Advantages

  • Musicians tend to be able to make music
  • MIDI is dependable, has been tested

Disadvantages

  • Large barrier to entry for non-musicians
  • Loses the idea of "music played by anyone"

Challenges

  • Combine these ideas into an easy-to-use interface that can be played by musicians and non-musicians alike
  • Decide whether music is generated acoustically or uses pre-recorded samples- must sound good!
  • Structure must be approachable, barrier to entry must be low
