Bench Power Supply

created by: Mark Kleback

This bench power supply was based on a schematic posted by Mike Colombo of Make Magazine. It varies between 0-24 Volts and provides 2 Amps of current.

Pictures

This bench power supply was specifically designed to be compatible with the banana cable plugs on my breadboard.
